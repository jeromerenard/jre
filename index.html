What I do

Mixing

Mixing is about recreating the listening experience that you once imagined when crafting your song.

I will take your song from being a promising rough mix to a mixed record that truly channels your vision and captures your emotions with a refreshing and assertive sound.

Recording & Producing

Recording is about making the most of your song and bring out their full potential.

By investing in the professioal production of your music, you will deliver a far better product to your public and fans.

Engineering

When working as an engineer, my approach is to make the raw recorded track sound as good as the artist idea and vision.

But Engineering is also fixing all kind of things and problems, setting up a session, a live or even a full studio.

Certified Dante IT Engineer.

Studio

Room

High-End hybrid setup.

Combining the power, speed and efficiency of the digital with the warmth and dynamic of the analog life.

Based around a ProTools HD,
the dante setup allows to route anything effortlessly to any possible connection between and inside any digital and analog point.

As final converter, I use a Burl ADC, allowing me to get that fatness, dynamic and width of the analog for the final print of your mix.
